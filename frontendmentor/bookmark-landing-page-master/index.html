Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can access them on the go.

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favourite sites.

Intelligent search

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time at all. No need to trawl through all of your bookmarks.

Share your bookmarks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with others. Create a shareable link that you can send at the click of a button.

Frequently Asked Questions

Bookmark is a clean and simple interface to organize your favourite websites. Open a new browser tab and see your sites load instantly. It's a browser extension that helps you manage your bookmarks more efficiently.

We're always looking to support more browsers. You can request support for a new browser by contacting our support team through the contact form below. We prioritize requests based on user demand and technical feasibility.

Currently, Bookmark is available as a browser extension for desktop browsers. We're exploring mobile app development and will announce any updates through our newsletter. Your bookmarks will sync across all your devices once we launch mobile support.

Bookmark should work with most Chromium-based browsers including Microsoft Edge, Brave, and Vivaldi. Since these browsers share the same extension architecture as Chrome, you can typically install the Chrome version of our extension. If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team.
